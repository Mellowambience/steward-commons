# Steward Commons Vision

## Mission

Steward Commons is an open protocol for preserving how ideas evolve.

It records not only what was decided, but why it was chosen, what alternatives were considered, what evidence mattered, what objections were raised, and what happened afterward.

## Vision

A world where knowledge compounds instead of fragments.

Today, much of humanity's reasoning disappears into meetings, chats, documents, model outputs, and memory. Steward Commons creates a durable structure for keeping judgment alive.

## The problem

Modern work produces artifacts faster than it preserves understanding. Teams ship conclusions, but lose context. AI systems generate answers, but often fail to preserve traceable reasoning. Institutions repeat mistakes because decision histories are scattered or invisible.

## The opportunity

If reasoning becomes portable, inspectable, and revisitable, then projects can learn across time. Future collaborators can understand the path, not just inherit the result.

## North star

Make every important idea stewardable: traceable, critique-able, forkable, revisitable, and accountable to outcomes.
