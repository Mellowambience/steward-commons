# Steward Commons Constitution

## Article 1 — Reasoning is a public good

Important decisions should preserve their context, alternatives, and consequences.

## Article 2 — Human agency comes first

AI may assist reasoning, but humans remain responsible for judgment, consent, and governance.

## Article 3 — Transparency beats false certainty

The protocol should expose assumptions, confidence, uncertainty, and dissent.

## Article 4 — Outcomes matter

A decision is not complete when it is made. It matures when its consequences are observed.

## Article 5 — Interoperability is essential

Steward Commons should be portable across tools, organizations, and implementations.
