# Governance

Steward Commons should be governed as an open protocol, not as a single application.

## Governance goals

- Protect interoperability
- Keep the protocol vendor-neutral
- Make changes transparent
- Preserve human agency
- Encourage plural implementations

## Proposal process

1. Submit a Steward Improvement Proposal.
2. Document motivation, tradeoffs, and alternatives.
3. Invite objections and revisions.
4. Record the decision and rationale.
5. Revisit the decision after adoption.
